Welcome to FPress

FPress is Wordpress without the bloat. I honestly got inspired by my good friend easrng, she created her own blogging software from the ground up called Maylog. I think it's an amazing take on blogs in general and is quite lightweight. I basically came up with the name because I was like fuck Wordpress. One of the great things about my solution, unlike Wordpress, it is entirely client side HTML/CSS with a sprinkle of JavaScript. JavaScript isn't even required, it's for the optional search and for the counter at the footer of this webpage. Maylog is a really cool idea kind of like Medium (kind of). You basically create blog entries by sending an email to the bot and it will be published to her domain easrng.us.to and anyone can view it and share it. Yeah, my blog isn't as sexy but that's the point. It also doesn't require a backend. Or even logins. If I wanted to I could host this on a waterproof cellphone dropped in a public bathroom's toilet water tank. I could edit this anywhere in the world and just FTP/commit the files to a server. I probably won't officialy release the code because this is too niche, but you could always yank it off my GitHub Repo. Anyway, this is MY solution to the blogging epidemic.
